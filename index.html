Comparative Advantage Score (CAS)

Algerian sectors are ranked across six integrated dimensions—resource availability, energy-cost advantage, labor supply, industrial anchors, policy environment, and AfCFTA export headroom. No other platform reconciles this breadth. CAS is machine-readable, version-controlled, and quarterly-refreshed.

Resource availability

Integrated DGD feeds quantify iron ore reserves, phosphates, hydrocarbons, and rare earths. Machine-readable, annually updated, with extraction feasibility scored against capex thresholds.

Energy-cost advantage

Sonelgaz tariff data reconciled with Bank of Algeria benchmarks. Industrial electricity costs indexed quarterly—critical for power-intensive sectors like aluminum, fertilizer, and steel.

Labor supply

ONS employment data by region and skill; ALCES apprenticeship capacity; diaspora labor-cost arbitrage. Scored for manufacturing, agro-processing, and tech-enabled services.

Industrial anchors

Existing clusters identified via customs data: automotive parts in Oran, food processing in Blida, metal fabrication in Annaba. Backward/forward linkage benefits quantified and de-risked.

Policy environment

Law 23-17 (land procedures), 2022 Free Zones Law, AAPI incentive regimes, and 2025 Mining Law amendments hard-coded. Regulatory friction scored and workarounds provided.

AfCFTA export headroom

Tariff concessions on Algerian exports to African markets quantified. First-mover advantage scored for sectors with low regional competition (agro-processing, light manufacturing).

Compliance Rules Decoder

Algerian investment rules are complex and frequently amended. We hard-code every provision—51/49 equity rules, AAPI incentive regimes, Free Zones Law workarounds, and Mining Law updates—into every opportunity package, version-controlled and machine-readable.

  • 51/49 Foreign Equity Rule (Investment Code)

    Foreign partners must hold ≤49% equity unless approved sectoral exception exists. Workarounds: diaspora investor status, Free Zones registration, or strategic sector designation. Each option machine-encoded with legal-structure recommendations and tax implications.

  • AAPI Incentives & Registration (National Agency for Development of Investment)

    Tax holidays (3–10 years), import duty exemptions, and land allocation conditional on investment size and sectoral classification. Eligibility auto-scored; timeline and documentation flagged before DFI appraisal.

  • Law 23-17: Land Procedures (2023)

    Procedures for industrial zone allocation, private land acquisition, and easement registration. Processing time: 6–12 months. Localize-DZ quantifies delay risk and alternative-site availability.

  • 2022 Free Zones Law

    Foreign equity rules suspended; 100% ownership permitted. Customs and VAT exemptions apply. Trade logistics data from Algiers, Oran, and Béjaïa ports integrated to score zone viability for import substitution and export-oriented operations.

  • 2025 Mining Law Amendments

    Phosphate and rare-earth ventures subject to new revenue-sharing formulas and local-content requirements. Localize-DZ updates models quarterly to reflect regulatory changes and benchmark precedent deals.

  • Foreign Exchange & Repatriation Rules (Central Bank)

    Dividend repatriation limits codified. Localize-DZ flags currency-risk exposure and models scenarios for hard-currency scarcity; integration with Bank of Algeria FX data ensures appraisal committees see real-time constraints.
